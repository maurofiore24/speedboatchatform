Common Questions

Before you ask us…

The five questions we get most. Everything else — just message us.

No license required. Our experienced captain drives the boat at all times. You just relax and enjoy the view. The captain knows every corner of the Bay of Kotor.
Maximum 7 persons per trip. The boat is comfortable for groups of 2–6. If you have a larger group, contact us and we can discuss options.
For Wedding & Photoshoot bookings — fuel is fully included. For Private Charters — there is a fuel surcharge of €50 per hour on top of the base rate, making the total €500/hour.
The default pickup is Kotor Riva (the main waterfront promenade). We can also arrange pickup from Perast, Porto Montenegro, Adriatica, Tivat, Porto Novi, Herceg Novi, and more. Just tell us your location when booking.
Use our booking form on this page to select your date, time, and route — then send the summary via WhatsApp or Instagram DM. We confirm personally within a few hours. No automated bots, real person on the other end.
